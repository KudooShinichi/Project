The benz patent-Motorwagen N1, the firstcar in the the world, was built by German Engeenier and Inventor Karl Benz.

In 1960, Karl Benz denoted his car to German Museum in Munich, Germany.

In 1936, three copies of car were built for the Mercedes Benz Museum, the Muscum of tecnology in Vienna and the Transport Museum in Dresden.
